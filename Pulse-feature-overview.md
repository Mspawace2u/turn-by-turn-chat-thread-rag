## 🧠 What is Pulse?

*   It’s a daily **proactive update/briefing** that ChatGPT builds overnight — based on your past chats, Memory, feedback, and optionally connected apps (calendar, email, etc.).   [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12293630-chatgpt-pulse?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   You’ll get visual “cards” in the morning that you can scan quickly or open to dig deeper.   [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-pulse/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   It suggests things it _thinks_ will be useful: meeting agendas, reminders, follow-ups on topics you’ve discussed, travel tips, etc.   [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-pulse/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   You get to “curate” it: thumbs up/down, tell it what you care about (or don’t), tune what you want to see.   [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12293630-chatgpt-pulse?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    

* * *

## ⚠️ Limitations + availability (for now)

*   **Only for Pro users**, and **only on mobile (iOS/Android)**. No desktop/web version yet.   [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12293630-chatgpt-pulse?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   Memory must be turned on (so ChatGPT “remembers” your context) for Pulse to work.   [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12293630-chatgpt-pulse?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   You can disable it or parts of it (turn off Pulse, disable memory, disconnect apps) in settings.   [OpenAI Help Center](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12293630-chatgpt-pulse?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
    
*   It’s still in **preview / experimental** mode. Things won’t always land perfectly.   [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-pulse/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

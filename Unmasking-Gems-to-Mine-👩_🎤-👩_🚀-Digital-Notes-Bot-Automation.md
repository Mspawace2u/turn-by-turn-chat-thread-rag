### Step 1: Setting Up Your Central Database in Notion

First, you'll need to create a place where all your notes will be stored. This will be your "central database" in Notion.

*   **Create a Notion Account**: Go to [Notion.so](https://www.notion.so/) and sign up for an account if you don’t already have one.

### Step 5: Searching in Notion

Notion has built-in search functionality that will let you search by keywords, topics, or phrases in your notes.

*   **Using Notion's Search**: Just use the search bar at the top of the Notion window to find any text within your database.

# UYDO Audit — Mini Pitch Deck (Clinic Transition — Corrected Authority Version)

## Slide 1 — You’ve already proven this works

**You built a full practice from scratch.**

You found patients.  
Helped them get results.  
Earned trust in a new country.

The clinic isn’t the beginning.

It’s the next evolution.

## Slide 2 — But the model is changing

Your traveling practice worked because everything stayed close to you.

Your schedule.  
Your patients.  
Your decisions.

A clinic introduces something new:

The business now has to operate beyond your immediate reach.

* * *

## Slide 3 — What worked before doesn’t scale the same way now

Not because it was wrong.

Because it was built for a different structure.

The workflows, tools, and support around you  
need to shift to match this version of the business.

Or friction slowly replaces freedom.

* * *

## Slide 4 — This is where most clinic transitions get heavy

Not in the treatment room.

Behind it.

Marketing that needs coordination.  
Support staff that need clarity.  
Systems that need structure.

Not more effort.

Better alignment.

* * *

## Slide 5 — What the UYDO Audit does

We look at everything supporting your clinic:

Your patient flow  
Your marketing setup  
Your support roles  
Your backend systems

And identify exactly:

What to keep  
What to adjust  
What to simplify  
What to build next

* * *

## Slide 6 — So the clinic runs cleaner than your travel model ever could

Not dependent on you being everywhere.

Not dependent on you figuring out every operational detail.

Structured so support actually supports you.

Not waits on you.

* * *

## Slide 7 — Why this stage matters most

You’re early enough in the clinic transition  
that small adjustments create massive long-term stability.

Instead of layering fixes later  
on top of a structure that was never designed for this model.

This is where you build it right.

Once.

* * *

## Slide 8 — What this gives you

Confidence that the business side matches the quality of your clinical work.

Clarity in what each person and system is responsible for.

Freedom to focus on patients  
without carrying the operational load mentally.

* * *

## Slide 9 — What working together looks like

You walk me through how your practice currently operates.

I map it, identify friction points, and show you the clearest path forward.

So your clinic grows on structure.

Not improvisation.

* * *

## Slide 10 — The outcome

A clinic that supports your expertise  
instead of quietly competing with it for your energy.

So you can scale the practice  
without scaling your stress.

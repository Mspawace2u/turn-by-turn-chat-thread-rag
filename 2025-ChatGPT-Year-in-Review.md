Not a palette. A **lane**.

*   2 neutrals (black, charcoal, cream, navy)

### 3️⃣ Fabric > Everything

Your nervous system wears the clothes.

*   Stretchy
    
*   Breathable
    
*   Soft or structured-but-forgiving

**Formula > creativity on low-spoon days**

*   Big top + slim pant + cool shoe

*   Matching set + “statement” layer

*   **Accessories live attached** (earrings stored with jacket)
    

Decision fatigue can’t catch you if you move faster than it.

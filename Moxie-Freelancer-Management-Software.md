### Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up an Open-Ended Project with Submissions in Moxie

#### Step 1: Create the Master Project

1.  **Log in to Moxie**: Start by logging into your Moxie account.
    
2.  **Navigate to Projects**: On the left-hand side menu, click on "Projects."
    
3.  **Create a New Project**:
    
    *   Click on the "+ New Project" button.
        
    *   Enter the project name, e.g., "Client Name - Ongoing Submissions."
        
    *   Set the project type to "Open-ended" or select the appropriate type that best describes your ongoing project.
        
    *   Click "Create" to save the project.
        

#### Step 2: Set Up the Client Portal

1.  **Access Client Management**:
    
    *   From the left-hand menu, go to "Clients."
        
    *   Find the client you are setting up the project for, or add a new client if they are not already in your system.
        
2.  **Enable Portal Access**:
    
    *   Click on the client’s profile.
        
    *   Ensure the "Portal Access" is enabled. If not, enable it and send the client an invitation to access the portal.
        
3.  **Customize Client Portal**:
    
    *   Go to "Settings" in the left-hand menu.
        
    *   Under "Client Portal Settings," customize the portal to include instructions and links for submissions.
        
    *   Ensure there is a section for "Submissions" where clients can easily find the form you will create.
        

#### Step 3: Create the Submission Form

1.  **Navigate to Forms**:
    
    *   On the left-hand menu, click on "Forms."
        
    *   Click on the "+ New Form" button.
        
2.  **Design the Form**:
    
    *   Give your form a title, e.g., "Ongoing Project Submission Form."
        
    *   Add the necessary fields:
        
        *   Text Field for "Assignment Title."
            
        *   Text Area for "Assignment Description."
            
        *   URL Field for "Link Submission."
            
    *   Customize any additional fields you might need.
        
3.  **Save and Share the Form**:
    
    *   Save the form.
        
    *   Go back to the Client Portal Settings and embed the form into the client portal.
        
    *   You can also provide a direct link to the form in the portal instructions.
        

#### Step 4: Automate and Organize Submissions

1.  **Create a Tagging System**:
    
    *   Go to "Settings" and find the "Tags" section.
        
    *   Create tags that will help you organize the submissions, e.g., "Assignment 1", "Assignment 2."
        
2.  **Set Up Custom Fields**:
    
    *   Under "Settings," navigate to "Custom Fields."
        
    *   Create custom fields relevant to your project, such as "Submission Date," "Status," etc.
        

#### Step 5: Workflow Automation

1.  **Set Up Automations**:
    
    *   Go to "Automations" in the left-hand menu.
        
    *   Click on "+ New Automation."
        
    *   Create an automation rule that triggers when a new form submission is received. For example:
        
        *   Trigger: New Form Submission
            
        *   Action: Update Project Status, Notify Team, or Apply Tag.
            
2.  **Configure Notifications**:
    
    *   In the same automation setup, configure email or in-app notifications for you and your client to keep everyone informed of new submissions and their statuses.
        

#### Step 6: Shared Workspace (Optional)

1.  **Integrate Shared Tools**:
    
    *   If Moxie allows integration with tools like Google Drive or Dropbox, go to "Integrations" in the Settings.
        
    *   Connect your chosen tool.
        
2.  **Create a Shared Folder**:
    
    *   Create a shared folder within the connected tool specifically for the client's submissions.
        
    *   Provide the client with access and instructions on how to upload their assignments here.
        
3.  **Link Shared Workspace in Client Portal**:
    
    *   Add a link to the shared folder within the client portal for easy access.
        

### Final Steps

*   **Test the Workflow**: Before going live, test the entire workflow by submitting a test form to ensure that everything is working as expected.
    
*   **Client Training**: Provide a brief training session or a guide for your client on how to use the portal and submit their assignments.
    
*   **Monitor and Adjust**: After the initial setup, monitor the process and make any necessary adjustments to improve efficiency.

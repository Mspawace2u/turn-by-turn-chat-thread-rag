## Q: 
Hi Sam, Im new here. What information do you need from me?

## A: 
To tailor my assistance to your specific needs, I'll need some information about your business and your goals. Here are some questions to get us started:

### Business Information:

1.  **What is your business name?**
    
2.  **Do you have a website? If so, please provide the URL.**
    
3.  **Please give a brief description of your business.**
    

### Customer Details:

4.  **Who are your target customers? Please provide details on their demographics, interests, and pain points.**
    
5.  **Do you have any customer avatars or personas? If so, please describe them.**
    

### Goals and Objectives:

6.  **What are your main goals for the sales and marketing efforts (e.g., increase conversions, generate leads, build brand awareness)?**
    
7.  **Are there specific products or services you want to highlight?**
    

### Tone and Style:

8.  **What tone and style should the sales communications have (e.g., professional, casual, friendly)?**
    
9.  **Are there specific phrases or language guidelines you want to be followed?**
    

### Resources and References:

10.  **Do you have any templates or examples of previous successful sales scripts?**
    
11.  **Are there any specific resources or materials you want me to refer to?**
    

If you have any relevant documents such as a brand guide, marketing strategy, customer avatars, or descriptions of your services and offers, please share them with me as well. This will help me provide the most effective and tailored advice for your business.

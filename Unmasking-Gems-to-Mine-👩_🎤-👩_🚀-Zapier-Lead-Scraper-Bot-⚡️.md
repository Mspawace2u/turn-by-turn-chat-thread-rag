Zapier can help you automate lead management by seamlessly connecting your lead generation tools (like Facebook Lead Ads, Google Forms, or LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms) with your CRM, email marketing software, or sales tools. Here are some ways you can use Zapier for lead management:

### **1. Capture & Organize Leads Automatically**

*   **Sync Leads to Your CRM**: Send new leads from forms (Typeform, Google Forms, Facebook Lead Ads, etc.) directly into HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, or another CRM.
    
*   **Store Leads in a Spreadsheet**: Automatically log new leads into Google Sheets or Airtable for easy tracking and reporting.
    
*   **Tag and Categorize Leads**: Use Zapier to filter and categorize leads based on responses before adding them to your CRM.
    

### **2. Follow Up with Leads Instantly**

*   **Send Automated Welcome Emails**: Connect your lead forms to Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, or ConvertKit to send a personalized email as soon as a lead submits a form.

*   **Send SMS Notifications**: Notify your sales team via Slack, SMS (Twilio), or Microsoft Teams when a new lead is captured.

### **4. Analyze & Report on Lead Performance**

        *   Interest (e.g., email opt-ins, resource downloads)
            
        *   Consideration (e.g., webinar sign-ups, product demos)
            
        *   Decision (e.g., cart checkout, direct inquiries)
            
        *   Retention (e.g., follow-ups, loyalty offers)

    *   For every "Yes" or "No" decision, map the next step or action:
        
        *   Example:
            
            *   **Yes** → Move to the next stage.
                
            *   **No** → Trigger a follow-up email or exit the funnel.

    *   Highlight specific events that signal progress (e.g., purchases, sign-ups).
        
    *   Example: If a lead adds an item to the cart but doesn't purchase, trigger an abandoned cart email.
        
5.  **Analyze Feedback Loops**
    
    *   Show where leads might re-enter the funnel:
        
        *   Retargeting ads.
            
        *   Reactivation campaigns for cold leads.

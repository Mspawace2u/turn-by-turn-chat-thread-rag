# Anti-Burnout AI Workflow Agent Blueprinter for NDs

Here are the 5 exact prompts you can copy, save to reuse whenever you want to turn a pile of random advice into a functional, BS-free internal workflow agent, test the outcomes, then deploy for beta:

### Prompt 1: The "Shoot Me Straight" Filter 

#### (Dig Out the Gold) 

*Use this when introducing your very first resource or idea.*

"I am adding a new resource for your review. Shoot me straight: extract only the high-value, actionable gold from this resource. Does it contain a true set of actionable steps that matches the promise in the headline or lead paragraph, or is it just theory/seeding a sale?

Filter your answer through my specific context as a 20-year B2B ops vet with AuDHD who wants to unmask, drop toxic positivity/productivity/one way to do it right or succeed in digital biz f\*ckery, and build a legacy without adding to internet noise'. I serve bootstrapped ND founders, solo owners, and leaders of tiny teams who struggle to scale profits without burning the f\*ck out and need executive function support that fits their available resources. They're often non-techie, systems averse, and are late adopters or new to AI and just want a way to get stuff accomplished that works with their brain."

### Prompt 2: The Critical Integration Rule 

#### (Summarize & Layer) 

*Use this when bringing in your second or third resource.*

"I am adding another resource with parallel, but potentially conflicting, info. Apply the exact same critical thinking as before. Tell me what makes sense to pursue and what I should ignore based on my specific intent and my fear of \[Insert friction point, e.g., wasting daylight on noise\]. If there are actionable gems, tell me exactly how you plan to integrate them into our strategy without short-circuiting its potential effectiveness or overwhelming me."

### Prompt 3: The Master Spec Generation 

#### (Merge into an Agent) 

*Use this when you have enough raw material and want to build the "Machine."*

"Generate a fully merged, updated Master Spec (Custom Instructions) for an LLM Agent based on the vetted insights we've gathered. Organize it into:

System Role & Objective My Context & Guardrails Core Editing/Processing Rules Specific Output Frameworks An Execution Checklist (include actionable advice that drives measurable outcomes but won't overwhelm me)." 

### Prompt 4: The Scope Adjustment 

#### (The Reality Check) 

*Use this if the agent gets too ambitious or complex, and you need a safe starting point*.

"Wait, that Master Spec is for 'V2 and beyond.' I need a prototype agent to test out right now so I can just start making progress and feel safe showing up. Scale the spec back. Give me a specific 'V1 Prototype' spec tailored ONLY to processing \[Insert specific starting material, e.g., raw voice memos\]. Filter out any strategy that causes friction or anxiety."

### Prompt 5: The Live Test 

#### (Test the Workflow) 

*Use this to officially launch your new agent.*

"The V1 Prototype Spec looks solid. Let's run a live test so I can try this workflow on for size. Adopt the persona and rules of the agent spec we just created. Here is my first raw input to process: \[Paste raw dump/input/intel\]."

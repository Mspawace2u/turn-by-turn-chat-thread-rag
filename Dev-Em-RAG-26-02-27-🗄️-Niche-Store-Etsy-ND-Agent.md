This is the only part that meaningfully reduces wasted effort. The promise of “real data, not guesswork” is mostly this step.

### 2) Use AI for **idea generation**, not for deciding what sells

**Action:** Prompt AI to generate a batch of short slogan/concept ideas tied to the validated niche.

**Useful format:**

**Action:** Pull professionally made vector artwork from a stock library instead of designing everything yourself.

**Useful criteria for artwork:**

*   Transparent background or POD-ready assets
    
*   Scalable/vector formats
    
*   Clean enough to use across multiple products
    
*   Easy to combine with text
    

**Why this matters:**  
This is one of the few real speed shortcuts in the video. If your goal is rapid testing, using ready-made art beats fiddling around pretending you’re suddenly a designer.

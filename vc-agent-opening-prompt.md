`I’m starting a new AI-powered microapp build to reduce a real workflow bottleneck.`

`Working app folder: /Volumes/Sandbox/agents/[app-name-here]`

`Reference resources folder: /Volumes/Sandbox/agents/resources`

`Read and follow:`

- `/Volumes/Sandbox/agents/resources/prompts/step-1-prompt.md`  
- `/Volumes/Sandbox/agents/resources/prompts/handoff-rule.md`  
- `/Volumes/Sandbox/agents/resources/system-rules.md`

`Context: I do not start blind. I usually begin with a high-level overview of:`

- `what the app is`  
- `what it does`  
- `what gap or bottleneck it fills`  
- `what workflow it improves or automates`

`Your job right now is to begin Step 1 only.`

`Start by asking me the Step 1 questions one at a time. Do not generate code yet. Do not jump to Step 2. Do not skip approval gates.`

`As a neurodivergent solo entrepreneur who is an Operations Strategist specializing in workflow optimization, keep these things in mind:`

1. `First, if I forgot, remind me to add the /resources folder to your workspace, so you can access it before Step 1 begins.`  
2. `Don’t use developer jargon. Explain functions and features in simple human language.`  
3. `I understand the general SPA file/component structure for my preferred stack, so do not over-explain basics.`  
4. `Long walls of text are frustrating. Use concise numbered lists, IFTTT logic, and only the minimum detail needed for action steps I need to take in order to move fast toward the UX/UI result I want you to create.`

## Q: 
how do I set up fast VPN on a Samsung Smart TV

## A: 
To set up FastVPN on your Samsung Smart TV, you can configure it directly on your router, share a VPN connection from your computer, or use a Smart DNS service. Each method has specific steps, such as accessing your router's settings to input FastVPN details, enabling a hotspot on your computer with VPN enabled, or changing your TV's DNS settings to those provided by FastVPN. For detailed instructions, refer to FastVPN's official guide.

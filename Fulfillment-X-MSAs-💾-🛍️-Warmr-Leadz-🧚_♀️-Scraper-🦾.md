*   Gate: exclude if `post_cadence_30d < 2` OR `last_post_age > 45d`.

## 7) MCP flow (lean + HITL)

1.  **Discover**
    
    *   Per channel job: search → scrape → push raw rows to `staging_sheet`.
        
2.  **Normalize + Score**
    
    *   Zapier step or tiny Cloudflare Worker: map fields, compute subscores & `partner_score`, write to `partners_master`.
        
3.  **De-dupe**
    
    *   Match on canonical URL/handle; keep highest `partner_score`.
        
4.  **HITL Review**
    
    *   View: filter `partner_score ≥ 62`, freshness < 30d → human thumbs-up (`status = approved`).
        
5.  **Outreach Pack**
    
    *   Generate `why_short` + load partner template (value proposition, audience match, proposed split).
        
6.  **Send & Track**
    
    *   Hand off to client’s CRM/DM workflow; update `status` + `next_step_date`.
        
7.  **Learn**
    
    *   Wins/losses feed back into weights (e.g., maybe reply rate > authority).

## 9) Risks & guardrails

*   **Platform ToS:** keep to public endpoints; throttle; avoid member-level data on FB groups.
    
*   **Data decay:** re-scrape “approved” records every 30 days; drop if stale.
    
*   **False positives:** require HITL review and short “why” before pitch.
    
*   **Deliverability:** never mass-email from shared domain; route via client’s CRM & warmed sender.

## Q: 
List available prompt frameworks

## A: 
Here are the available **prompt frameworks** that you can choose from:

* * *

### ✅ **Core Prompt Frameworks Available**

1.  **RTF: Role - Task - Format**
    
    *   **Role**: Who the AI should be.
        
    *   **Task**: What it needs to do.
        
    *   **Format**: How the output should be structured.
        
2.  **TAG: Task - Action - Goal**
    
    *   **Task**: What needs to be done.
        
    *   **Action**: How it should be done.
        
    *   **Goal**: The ultimate outcome.
        
3.  **BAB: Before - After - Bridge**
    
    *   **Before**: Describe the current situation/problem.
        
    *   **After**: Describe the desired state/outcome.
        
    *   **Bridge**: Ask for how to get from before to after.
        
4.  **APE: Action - Purpose - Expectation**
    
    *   **Action**: What to do.
        
    *   **Purpose**: Why it needs to be done.
        
    *   **Expectation**: What the result should look like.
        
5.  **RACE: Role - Action - Context - Expectation**
    
    *   **Role**: Who the AI is acting as.
        
    *   **Action**: What the AI should do.
        
    *   **Context**: Background or scenario.
        
    *   **Expectation**: Desired form or quality of output.
        
6.  **COAST: Context - Objective - Actions - Scenario - Task**
    
    *   **Context**: Set the scene.
        
    *   **Objective**: Goal to achieve.
        
    *   **Actions**: Required actions.
        
    *   **Scenario**: Situation being addressed.
        
    *   **Task**: Final task for the AI.
        
7.  **RISE: Role - Input - Steps - Expectation**
    
    *   **Role**: Define AI’s persona or role.
        
    *   **Input**: Information or content it will use.
        
    *   **Steps**: Ask for step-by-step generation.
        
    *   **Expectation**: End result format or goal.
        
8.  **TRACE: Task - Request - Action - Context - Example**
    
    *   **Task**: Core task at hand.
        
    *   **Request**: What the user wants.
        
    *   **Action**: What AI should do.
        
    *   **Context**: Situation info.
        
    *   **Example**: Give a model example.
        
9.  **ERA: Expectation - Role - Action**
    
    *   **Expectation**: End result.
        
    *   **Role**: Who the AI is.
        
    *   **Action**: What the AI should do.
        
10.  **ROSES: Role - Objective - Scenario - Expected Solution - Steps**
    

*   **Role**: AI persona.
    
*   **Objective**: What needs to be solved or created.
    
*   **Scenario**: Realistic setup.
    
*   **Expected Solution**: What the answer should look like.
    
*   **Steps**: Guide to reach the solution.
    

11.  **CARE: Context - Action - Result - Example**
    

*   **Context**: Setting or situation.
    
*   **Action**: What needs to be done.
    
*   **Result**: Desired outcome.
    
*   **Example**: Illustrative sample.
